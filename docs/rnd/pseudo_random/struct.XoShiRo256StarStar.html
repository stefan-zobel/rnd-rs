Expand description

256-bit xoshiro256** pseudo random generator suggested by David Blackman and Sebastiano Vigna (2019). It is about 40% faster than XorShift64Star despite having a 4 times larger state space.

This generator has a period of 2256 − 1.

This generator is 4-dimensionally equidistributed.

This generator is almost as fast as Stc64.
